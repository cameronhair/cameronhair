    About Cameron

    From the streets of New York to the heart of Saint Petersburg, I've passionately styled hair since 1991. With over 30 years of experience, Cameron Hair has become a destination of style and expertise. As a color specialist, my artistic eye thrives on revealing the perfect hues that complement your unique features. From corrective coloring challenges to crafting effortless haircuts tailored to your lifestyle, my goal is simple: ensuring you look incredible even on your most low-maintenance days. Offering versatile styling options and using Matrix Haircare Products, I prioritize your hair's long-term health and deliver exceptional results. Let's embark on a creative journey together, where I'll weave artistry into every evening look, making you feel truly one-of-a-kind.
